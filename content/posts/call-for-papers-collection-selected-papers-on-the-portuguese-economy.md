The *Portuguese Economic Journal* welcomes submissions to its [collection “Selected papers on the Portuguese Economy”](https://link.springer.com/collections/difgdbgcfg) edited by Rui Albuquerque (Carroll School of Management, Boston College). The deadline is **31 January 2026**, the submission guidelines are available [here](https://link.springer.com/journal/10258/submission-guidelines), and manuscripts can be submitted [here](https://www.editorialmanager.com/pejo/default.aspx).
